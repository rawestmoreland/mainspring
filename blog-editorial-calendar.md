## Notes on Execution

**Quick wins to publish first:** Posts #1, #2, #3 have the highest search volume and directly demonstrate Hairspring's value prop. Prioritize these in Month 1.

**Update annually:** Posts #16, #24, #31, #45 are designed to be refreshed each year — same URL, updated content. This compounds SEO authority over time.

**Internal linking hubs:** Post #47 (Glossary) and Post #2 (Beginner's Guide) should be linked from nearly every other post. Build these out robustly.

**CTA strategy:** Mechanics posts (#1, #5, #6, #15, #25, #30, #39, #41, #42) are highest-conversion — include a direct Hairspring CTA at the end of each. Other categories warrant a softer mention.
